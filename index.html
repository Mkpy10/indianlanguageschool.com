Manchester Trip
Won first prize in basketball at Manchester

Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 1982

The school library is well stocked with over 30,000 fiction and non-fiction titles and periodicals in English, Hindi, Sanskrit and French.

School Sports

Was established in 1982

Students are given coaching for Swimming, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, Table tennis and Football. Facilities are also provided for Chess, Carom, Gymnastics, Taekwondo and Yoga.

School Art

Was established in 1982

The school has three well-equipped Art Rooms and a lot of encouragement is given to students to develop their artistic abilities. Besides, drawing and painting, students are also taught several crafts.
